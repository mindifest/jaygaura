The Proof is in the Garage Sale

Aug 13, 2024

We, humans, are driven by deep desires, but what we truly seek is personal fulfillment, not just the fleeting satisfaction of our wants. How do we know this? Take a look at the American tradition of “garage sales”—a sale of items we once craved, yet no longer need or care for. It’s proof that mere desire fulfillment isn’t the endgame; it’s a temporary stop on the journey toward real, lasting happiness.
